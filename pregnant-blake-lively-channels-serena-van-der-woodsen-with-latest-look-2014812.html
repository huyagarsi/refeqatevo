Pregnant Blake Lively Channels Serena van der Woodsen With Latest Look

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Serena van der Woodsen, eat your heart out!

Blake Lively took to the streets of NYC on Monday, Dec. 8, looking just as radiant as she has throughout her entire pregnancy. But more so than ever before, Lively’s latest ensemble looked like it could have been pulled straight out of her ultra-chic Gossip Girl character’s closet!

Mrs. Ryan Reynolds, 27, was spotted in Manhattan’s Union Square neighborhood in a slate sweater, fur-trimmed charcoal cape, and leather J Brand leggings. Sky-high booties, which she strolled in with ease despite her expanded frame, and a Celine trapeze purse finished the outfit. (Oh, and she wore this glam getup just to stop by The City Bakery, as you do.)

If the Preserve CEO’s outfit appeared familiar, that’s because two of the pieces are available on her lifestyle site, courtesy of her arsenal of underground designers. The cape, which she used to bundle her baby bump, is by Alicia Adams Alpaca ($895, Preserve.us), and the stacked golden cuffs are by Mikal Winn (from $132 to $144, Preserve.us).

For those counting at home, the Alicia Adams Alpaca piece is the fifth cape Lively’s worn publicly since announcing her pregnancy in October, surely thanks to the way the toppers can accommodate her ever-changing shape. “I’m going to amp up my style by wearing things that are stretchier, ’cause that’s all that fits!” Lively previously joked.

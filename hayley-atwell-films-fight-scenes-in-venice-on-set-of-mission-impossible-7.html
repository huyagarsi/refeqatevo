Tom Cruise &amp; Hayley Atwell Film Intense Action Scenes on Top of a Moving Train for 'Mission: Impossi

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Tom Cruise and Hayley Atwell are leaping into action!

The 58-year-old actor and the 39-year-old actress were once again pictured on the set of the upcoming Mission: Impossible 7 on Thursday (April 22) in Yorkshire, United Kingdom.

PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of Tom Cruise

The duo has been filming some pretty intense scenes involving a moving train for the past few days. They got an early start to the shoot, beginning their action scenes before 8 a.m. on the train tracks.

In the last shot of the day, Tom Cruise was seen climbing on top of the train carriage while moving. At one point, it looked as though Tom was in real-life hero mode, as he was seen pulling a cameraman to safety atop the moving train.

Michael Buble's Wife Luisiana Lopilato Is Pregnant Again!

July 2024 · 2 minute read

They haven't met you yet — but they already love you! Michael Buble shared a very special announcement with his Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram followers on Monday, July 6: He and his wife, Luisana Lopilato, are expecting their second child together!

The big reveal actually came courtesy of the couple's son, 22-month-old Noah, who delivered the news in a video posted to the "Home" crooner's social media accounts. In the clip, Lopilato lies on the floor with her shirt pulled up to expose her bare stomach, and Buble asks, "Who's that in there?"

On cue, Noah — in a pair of Minions-themed pajamas, natch — walks over to his mom, places his tiny hand on her belly, and says, "Baby," as Britney Spears' "Oops…I Did It Again" plays in the background.

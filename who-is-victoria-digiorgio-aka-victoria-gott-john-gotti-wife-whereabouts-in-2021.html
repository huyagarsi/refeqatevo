Who Is Victoria DiGiorgio AKA Victoria Gotti? John Gotti Wife

Victoria DiGiorgio aka Victoria Gotti is mostly known for being the wife of a gangster, John Gotti who died on June 10, 2002, due to throat cancer. 

The relationship between Victoria and the crime boss began in New York City, where they first met at a bar in 1958. 

For around four years, their love blossomed, and finally decided to tie the knot of foreverness on March 6, 1962. 

Despite knowing the fact that her husband was a gangster, Victoria stuck with him regardless. Though her husband was a crime boss from outside, DiGiorgio always thought he was a principled man with deep family values. 

Although Gotti had a lot of wealth, he always chooses to live a middle-class lifestyle, as per his wife’s statement. 

Apart from this, while he was serving his life in a prison term in 1998, Gotti was diagnosed with throat cancer, which led him to his death on June 10, 2002.

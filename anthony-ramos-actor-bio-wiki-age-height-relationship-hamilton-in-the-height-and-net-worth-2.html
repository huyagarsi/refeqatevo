Anthony Ramos Actor

Ramos began his acting career in 2011 in various regional and touring musical productions. In 2012, Anthony featured in the production of ‘In the Heights’ during the Sony de la Vega tour. Later on, in 2014, Anthony performed in Heart and Lights at Radio City Music Hall alongside the Rockettes. However, Heart and Lights were canceled. At the time, Anthony had auditioned for the production of Hamilton at the Public Theater.

Also, Anthony has had the role of Justin Laboy in 21 Chump Street, a Lin-Manuel Miranda short musical film. On the other hand, Ramos was cast in director Spike Lee’s Netflix comedy-drama series, She’s Gotta Have It. He played the role of Mars Blackmon.

Anthony Ramos Singing

In October 2017, Ramos was one of the vocalists on Lin-Manuel Miranda’s song, ‘Almost Like Praying’. ‘Almost Like Praying’ was a released song to benefit Hurricane Maria victims. His first EP, The Freedom EP, included the two tracks ‘Freedom’ and ‘Common Ground’ that he released on November 8, 2017.

His debut album was produced by Will Wells who had worked with Logic and Pentatonix. Additionally, his album, The Freedom Ep, was released on the first anniversary of President Donald Trump’s inauguration, on January 20, 2018.

Also, the album includes three more songs; ‘Alright’, ‘When The Bell Tolls’, and ‘PRayer’. Anthony was announced to have joined Republic Records on June 13, 2019. His signing was documented on his YouTube series, It Takes A Village. His first album, The Good & The Bad, was released on October 25th, 2019. The album appeared at #21 on the US Billboard, Heatseekers Albums chart.

Anthony Ramos Hamilton

In early 2015, Hamilton opened its broadcast, and Ramos had the dual roles as John Laurens and Alexander Hamilton, Philip Hamilton’s elder son. Later on, Hamilton was moved to Broadway on July 13, 2015 in previews and opened on August 6th, 2015. Anthony left Hamilton production on November 20th, 2016.

Ramos is cast to play the dual roles of John Laurens and Phillip Hamilton in the upcoming musical drama film, Hamilton. Hamilton is based on the 2014 Broadway musical of Hamilton. Additionally, it is inspired by Alexander Hamilton’s biography.

Anthony Ramos In the Heights

Ramos is cast to play the role of  Usnavi de la Vega. The film is based on the stage musical of the same name by Hudes and Lin-Manuel Miranda.

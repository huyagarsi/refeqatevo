Tyler-Justin Anthony Sharpe, known as Lil Tecca, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer. He became famous with the release of single "Ransom", which reached number 4 on the Billboard Hot 100. The single was included in his debut mixtape We Love You Tecca, reaching number 4 on the US Billboard 200. . Mixtape also includes the singles "Love Me" and "Did It Again". His first studio album, Virgo World, debuted at No. 10 on the Billboard 200 and includes Billboard Hot 100 songs "Dolly" and "When You Down".

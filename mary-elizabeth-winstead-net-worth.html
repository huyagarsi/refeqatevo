What are some of her most famous roles?

Who is Mary Elizabeth Winstead married to?

She is married to actor Ewan McGregor. The couple got married in 2022 and share a son named Laurie.

What is her role in the Star Wars universe?

Winstead plays Hera Syndulla, a general in the rebellion, in the Disney+ series Ahsoka. Her character is well-loved by fans and is expected to have a more significant role in the show’s upcoming second season.

How did Winstead start her career?

She began her acting career with guest roles on TV shows like Touched by an Angel and Promised Land. Her first major role was as Jessica Bennett on the NBC soap opera Passions from 1999 to 2000.

Has she won any awards?

She has been nominated for several awards and won the Independent Spirit Award for Best Female Lead for her role in Smashed. She also received a Scream Award nomination for her performance in Black Christmas.

What is A Gentleman in Moscow about, and what is her role?

A Gentleman in Moscow is a TV series adaptation of the novel by Amor Towles. Winstead stars as Anna Urbanova, an actress and the love interest of the main character, Alexander Rostov, played by Ewan McGregor. The show follows Rostov’s life under house arrest in a hotel after the Russian Revolution.

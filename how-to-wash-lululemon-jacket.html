How do you wash a Lululemon jacket?

We recommend: machine wash cold with similar fabrics. avoid fabric softeners and abrasive fabrics/materials. lay flat to dry.

We recommend:

  • Cold wash, tumble dry low.
  • Close all zipper and Velcro® closures.
  • Use the least amount of detergent possible to clean it effectively—leftover detergent may inhibit the water-repellency.

    • Can I put my Lulu jacket in the dryer?

    Lululemon recommends to machine wash cold and air dry most of their clothes. Never put lululemon clothes in the dryer. Heat from the dryer will break down the elastic fibers in the fabrics, which will cause them to lose their stretch retention. Lululemon recommends not using fabric softener with their fabrics.
